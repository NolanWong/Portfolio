how to make Orange Chicken but better

Orange chicken was a dish popularised by Panda Express. to make the sauce, (the only thing unique to this dish) you should bring 3/4 cup water, 1/3 cup plus 1 tablespoon of soy sause, 1/4 cup white vinegar, and the zet and juice of an orange to a boil.

After it reaches a boil, bing it down to medium heat and let simmer for 8-10 mins add 1tbs water and cornstarch mixed seperately and mix for about 1 min and add in garlic ginger and jalapeno to steep.
