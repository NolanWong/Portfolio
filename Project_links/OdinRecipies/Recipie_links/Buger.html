Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • garlic powder
  • onions
  • pickles
  • pickled jalapeños
  • salt
  • pepper
  • buns

There are only two steps in this guide.

  1. Mix seasonings into the meat, then cook.
  2. Stack sandwich in this order:
    bottom bun, pickles, patty, onion, jalapeños, and top bun.
