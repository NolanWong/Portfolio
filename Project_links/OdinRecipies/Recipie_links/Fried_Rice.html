Ingredients

  • Carrot x1, minced
  • Cucumber x2, sliced and then quartered
  • spring onion x3 sliced
  • Egg x2
  • Chicken, one breast, bite sized pieces.
  • soy sauce, 2 tbs.
  • Rice 3 bowl, LEFTOVER
  • Garlic 4 cloves
  • ginger(small amount) ½ knob
  • Jalapeño x3
  • Edamame ¼ cup
  • Canola oil


PART 1-prep

Cook rice 1 day before
Dice cucumbers and carrot
Mince garlic, ginger and jalapeño
Cut up spring onion, and chicken
NOTE:cut veggies before meat.

PART 2-veggies and meat

Cook the chicken on a pan heated to medium-high heat for ~30-40 secs each side.
Add cucumber, jalapeño, carrot, and spring onion whites to a pan (save the greens for later.)
Once the jalapeños are getting soft add garlic and ginger, mix
Add chicken once onions are turning clear
Once chicken is cooked through, Add rice and mix.
Scoot rice to one side of the pan, and crack an egg in on the other side, scramble and combine (the rice should be coated in the egg.) After fully combining, repeat for the second egg
Add in edamame, mix and cook for 20 seconds
Cook and mix in soy sauce until chicken is cooked through

When serving, add spring onion greens on top of rice
